Basic Questions
1. What is SimStocks?

SimStocks is a virtual stock market simulation platform designed to provide users with an immersive experience in trading stocks without the risk of using real money. It offers a realistic environment where users can practice buying, selling, and trading stocks using virtual funds.

2. How does SimStocks work?

SimStocks operates as a real-time virtual stock market, mirroring the dynamics of the US stock market. Upon registration, users are allocated virtual funds, typically $10,000, which they can invest in a wide range of stocks listed on major exchanges.

3. Is there any cost associated with using SimStocks?

No, SimStocks is completely free to use. Users can sign up for an account and start trading stocks immediately without any fees or charges.
